About Us


Nestled in the heart of Naval Biliran, Neth's Café is more than just a coffee shop it's a cozy haven for coffee lovers and anyone seeking a friendly, relaxing spot. We believe that a great cup of coffee can bring people together, spark creativity, and brighten your day.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a place where everyone feels welcome to enjoy top-notch coffee and delicious treats. Our baristas are passionate about crafting each drink, whether it's a classic espresso, a rich latte, or one of our unique seasonal blends. Every cup is made with high quality, carefully sourced beans, ensuring a full, authentic flavor in every sip.

At Neth's Café, we’re dedicated to creating a memorable experience with each visit. Whether you're here for a quiet moment, catching up with friends, or finding inspiration for your next big idea, we’re here to make your day just a bit better.
